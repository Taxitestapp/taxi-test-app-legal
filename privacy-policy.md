# Privacy Policy for Taxi Test Study App

**Last updated:** 2025

Taxi Test Study App (“the App”) is operated by Taxi Test App Ltd (“we”, “us”, “our”).

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK and EU law.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to operate with **minimal personal data**. We do **not** require an account or login.

We only collect:

### 1.1 Local Device Data (Stored Only on Your Phone)
- Flashcard progress (traffic-light status)
- Mock test results
- Selected city
- In-app preferences

This data **never leaves your device**.

### 1.2 Payment Data
All subscription payments are processed securely by:
- **Apple App Store (Apple Payments)**  
- **Google Play Store (Google Payments)**  

We **do not** receive or store:
- card numbers  
- billing addresses  
- payment details  

Only Apple/Google handle the transaction.

### 1.3 App Analytics (Optional)
We may use anonymous analytics (e.g. Expo Insights or similar) to understand:
- number of app launches  
- crashes and errors  
- basic device information (model, OS version)  

No personal identifiers are collected.

---

## 2. How We Use Data
We use local and anonymised data to:
- provide core app functionality  
- improve study features  
- fix bugs  
- ensure mock tests behave as expected

We do **not**:
- sell data  
- track you across other apps  
- share your data with third parties  

---

## 3. Subscriptions
Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple and Google.

You can:
- cancel anytime in your device settings  
- view renewal dates  
- request refunds via Apple/Google  

We do not directly manage billing.

---

## 4. Children’s Privacy
This app is not intended for children under 13.  
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

---

## 5. Data Security
We take reasonable steps to protect your data.  
Because most data is stored **offline on your device**, you remain in full control.

Deleting the app deletes your data.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy.  
The “Last Updated” date will always be shown at the top.
